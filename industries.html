OUR PRODUCT CATEGORIES

Pawali Components

PRODUCT APPLICATIONS

Our industrial components are designed for specific applications in utensil manufacturing and metal forming processes.

Utensil Manufacturing Components

Our specialized components are essential for efficient utensil manufacturing processes. From balti mouth stretching to pawali bidding, each component is precision-engineered for optimal performance and durability in high-volume production environments.

Key Products:
  • C.I. Tapper Buffers (14", 17")
  • Pawali Bhavra (5", 6")
  • Balti Punching Bhingri (100/35 MM)
  • Balti Bidding Bearings (90 Diameter)
  • Balti Farm Cutters (15°, 30°)

Metal Forming Components

We manufacture precision components for various metal forming applications including spinning, pressing, and rolling processes. Our products are made from quality materials like WPS Iron, M.S., and tempered rolls to ensure longevity and consistent performance.

Key Products:
  • Spinning Machine Components
  • Press Machine Mendrills
  • Rolling Rolls (Various Sizes)
  • Bidding Rolls & Bearings
  • Specialized Cutters & Tools
