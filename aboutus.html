About Dinesh Industries

Manufacturers of precision industrial components and machinery since 1995

Our Story

Established in 1995, Dinesh Industries has evolved from a small workshop to a leading manufacturer of industrial components and machinery. Our journey began with a commitment to quality and precision in manufacturing industrial tools and components.

Today, we specialize in producing high-quality industrial components including C.I. Tapper Buffers, Pawali Bhavra, Balti Punching Bhingri, and various specialized rolls and bearings used in utensil manufacturing and metal forming industries.

Our dedication to precision engineering, use of quality materials like WPS Iron and M.S., and focus on customer needs has established us as a trusted partner for industries across India and beyond.

Our Mission & Vision

Driving industrial excellence through precision engineering

Our Mission

To manufacture high-precision industrial components and machinery that enhance manufacturing efficiency and productivity for our clients in utensil manufacturing and metal forming industries.

We achieve this through skilled craftsmanship, quality materials, and continuous improvement in our manufacturing processes to deliver reliable and durable products.

Our Vision

To become the preferred manufacturer of industrial components and machinery, recognized for our precision engineering, product reliability, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

We envision expanding our product range and geographical reach while maintaining our core values of quality, integrity, and innovation in industrial manufacturing.
